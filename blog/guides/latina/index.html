Tips

  • Best time to visit: April through September. Summers are hot, coast gets packed in August.
  • You need a car: Public transport between towns is slow and unreliable. Rent a car or bring yours.
  • Festivals: Most towns run sagre (food festivals) from June to September. Check local calendars.
  • Beaches: The most beautiful beaches with crystal-clear water are found in Sabaudia, Gaeta, Sperlonga, and San Felice Circeo
