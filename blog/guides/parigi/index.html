Tips

  • Museums: Many museums and cultural sites offer free or discounted admission if you are under 26 and a citizen of a European Union member state.
  • Getting around: I recommend buying the Navigo Pass. This rechargeable card allows you to travel on the metro, the RER, the funicular, the buses and trams in Paris and Ile-de-France, or the Orlybus and Roissybus airport buses. This ticket does not require photo identification, is quick to obtain, is rechargeable, is non-nominal, and can be purchased online before departure. You can load single tickets, a carnet, or a season ticket onto the pass at any time.
  • Coworking: Specified coworkings have free admissions but with mandatory consumption, while others have an admission charge.
