Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jun 19, 2026

This notice describes how Fabrizio Cafolla (fabriziocafolla.com) processes users’ personal data. The site does not use any analytics or tracking tools. The only processing of personal data concerns the optional newsletter subscription.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Fabrizio Cafolla, owner of this blog. For any privacy-related requests, you can contact me via LinkedIn.

2. Data Collected

  • Browsing data: No analytics, profiling, or tracking data is collected from users visiting the site. The site does not use third-party analytics tools.
  • Newsletter data: If you subscribe to the newsletter, the email address you provide is collected for the sole purpose of sending communications about new articles.

3. Purpose and Legal Basis of Processing

3.1 Newsletter

Processing of newsletter subscription data is based on your explicit consent pursuant to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR and Art. 130 of Italian Legislative Decree 196/2003 (Privacy Code).

  • Sending newsletter communications about new articles published on the blog
  • Managing subscription and unsubscription requests
  • Compliance with legal obligations related to electronic communications

4. Cookies

The site does not use analytics, profiling, or marketing cookies. Only strictly necessary technical cookies may be used to ensure the site functions correctly.

5. Data Processing and Third Parties

5.1 Newsletter - Brevo

Newsletter subscription data is processed through Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), a marketing automation platform. By subscribing to the newsletter, you agree that your personal information will be transferred to Brevo for processing in accordance with Brevo's Privacy Policy. Brevo acts as a data processor and processes data exclusively on our behalf and according to our instructions. Data transfer to Brevo is based on adequate safeguards as required by GDPR.

6. Users' Rights

Regarding newsletter subscription data, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in emails or contacting us directly. You may also request access, rectification, or deletion of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact me using the contact details available on the site. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).
