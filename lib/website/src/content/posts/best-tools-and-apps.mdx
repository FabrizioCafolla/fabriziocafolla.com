### [Wanderlog](https://wanderlog.com/)

**What is it for**: It allows you to plan your trip by making it easy to organize, schedule, and share the itinerary, places to visit, and the cost of each activity. It is a collaborative platform that also allows you to add other people, or to view other peopleâ€™s travel itineraries.

**Cost**: Free

### [Splitwise](https://www.splitwise.com/)

**What is it for**: It is an application useful for splitting expenses with other people. After a user records an expense, the application calculates how much each person owes to the others. It can also keep track of payments to ensure that everyone is even. This makes splitting expenses simple and stress-free.

**Cost**: Free

**Alternatives**: Tricount

## Payments

### [Revolut](https://revolut.com/referral/?referral-code=fabrizkc6s%21FEB1-24-AR-DXSD7-4-L1)

**What is it for**: It is a digital banking service that offers a wide variety of financial services. Its utility is that money transfers between Revolut accounts are free, currency exchange is commission-free (up to a maximum of 1000 euros during the week), and it allows the creation of virtual cards. Lastly, it ensures maximum security as it can be blocked at any time and a new card can be requested immediately. Moreover, by being able to load money directly with Google Pay or Apple Pay, you can easily add funds at any time without exposing your cards linked to your bank account.

**Cost**: Free or Paid Plan

**Alternatives**: N26, Hype, Wise

## Moving

### [Organic Maps](https://organicmaps.app/)

**What is it for**: detailed maps showing services, restaurants, dirt routes, and transportation. The most useful feature is the offline map that allows you to be able to find your way around and get around even without the Internet.

**Alternatives**: Google Maps, Open Street Map
